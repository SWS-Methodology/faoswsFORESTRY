Migration of FORESTRY PRODUCTION AND TRADE Statistical Processes into the SWS

Current FORESTRY methodology

The book focuses on SWS statistical processes on progress. However, it is important to highlight the current statistical value chain carried by FOA to deliver timely FAOSTAT annual Forestry Production and Trade data disseminations.

Data Collection

  1. FORESTRY PRODUCTION AND TRADE data collection relies on FAO official questionnaires and COMTRADE;

Operationnalty, the FAO-FOA is directly responsible for the send out of about 100 questionnairies per year, one questionnaire per country. For the rest of state members, the questionnaire sendout task is shared with international partners: - ITTO : The International Tropical Timber Organization - Eurostat : European Statistics - UNECE : The United Nations Economic Comission for Europe

  1. As of January of 2019, 107 countries have nominated AQUASTAT national correspondents as requested by FAO Land and Water Division between April-May 2018;

  2. Alternate correspondents from different agencies are also used to improve the data collection;

  3. The FAO Office of the Chief Statistician (OCS) has endorsed the annual Water and Agriculture questionnaire;

  4. AQUASTAT sends out the questionnaire in the first semester of each year.
